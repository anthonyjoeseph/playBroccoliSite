Focusing on the Culture
We collect traditional stories from all over the world. Our mission is to let children learn about different cultures and customs freely without any limitations. We believe understanding each other is the first step to uniting diverse people into one community.
Our books are available in six languages. The languages include English, Spanish, French, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean. We also provide audio in all six languages.

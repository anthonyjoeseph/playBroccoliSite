Focusing on the Culture
We are a New York based start-up company. We collect traditional stories from all over the world. Our mission is to educate children about different cultures and let them know that reading is another fun activity that they can do on digital devices. We believe that understanding each other is the first step to uniting diverse people into one community.
Our books are available in six languages. The languages include English, Spanish, French, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean. We also provide audio in all six languages!
